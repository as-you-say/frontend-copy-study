HOW IT
WORKS

You will work in a team of 3 graphic designers with rich experience. Most often you will design web user interfaces and microsites visuals. The workflow will include everything from prototype design, concept design to detailed graphic design , which you pass to a web developer to fine-tune animations, responsiveness, and other elements.

Sometimes you go to a photo shoot or shoot a video. We regularly shoot campaigns in Austria. There will be also days when we will need you for a video postproduction or an animation and motion graphic design.
